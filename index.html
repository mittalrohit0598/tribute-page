Straw Hats Crew

Achievements of the Straw Hat Pirates:

East Blue:

  • Goat Island: Responsible for freeing Koby from Alvida
  • Shells Town: Freed Shells Town by defeating Captain Morgan of the marines and saving Zoro and Koby. The first crew member joins the Straw Hat Pirates, the swordsman, Roronoa Zoro.
  • Organ Islands: Freed Orange Town by defeating the Buggy Pirates. The second crew member joins the Straw Hat Pirates, the navigator, Nami.
  • Gecko Islands: Stopped Kuro's plans of killing Kaya and inheriting her fortune. The third crew member joins the Straw Hat Pirates, the sniper, Usopp. They also recieve their first ship as a gift from Kaya, the Going Merry.
  • Baratie: Involved in defending the Baratie by defeating Krieg. The fourth crew member joins the Straw Hat Pirates, the cook, Sanji.
  • Conomi Islands: Defeated the Arlong Pirates to save Nami and freed Conomi Islands. The Straw Hats also regained their navigator, and Luffy gained his first bounty.
  • Loguetown: Escaped the Marines led by Captain Smoker and successfully set sail for the Grand Line.
  • Reverse Mountain: Passed through the Reverse Mountain to enter the Grand Line.

Paradise:

  • Cactus Island: Zoro defeats 100 bounty hunters at Whiskey Peak. Vivi joins the Straw Hats.
  • Little Garden: Saved Dorry and Brogy from the Mr. 3 team.
  • Drum Island: Defeated Wapol and saved Drum Kingdom. The fifth crew member joins the Straw Hat Pirates, the doctor, Tony Tony Chopper.
  • Alabasta: Saved Alabasta and defeated Crocodile. This caused the World Government (and the world itself) to fully take notice of the Straw Hats, with Luffy and Zoro receiving new bounties. The sixth crew member joins the Straw Hat Pirates, the archaeologist, Nico Robin.
  • Jaya: Defeated Bellamy and set sail for Sky Island.
  • Skypiea: Stopped a 400 year old war and defeated Enel.
  • Long Ring Long Land: Defeated Foxy in the Davy Back Fight. Fought Aokiji to stop him from hurting Robin.
  • Water 7: Saved Iceburg and Paulie from the attack of CP9. Ussop and Robin temporarily leave the crew.
  • Enies Lobby: Raided Enies Lobby to save Robin and Franky, declared war on the World Government and defeated CP9. The blueprints were burned, which destroyed the World Government's attempt to raise an ancient weapon to end the pirate age, and they needed Robin so they can get her to tell them where the ancient weapon is, but failed to retrieve her. After losing their first ship, the Going Merry, due to damage it took, the Straw Hat Pirates get a new one, the Thousand Sunny, and a shipwright, the cyborg Franky, not to mention reobtaining their sniper and archaeologist. They also encountered the legendary Marine, and Luffy's grandfather, Vice-Admiral Monkey D. Garp, as well as Koby and Helmeppo, and successfully escaped from them. Luffy's father is revealed to be the most wanted man, The Revolutionary, Monkey D. Dragon. The entire crew also earned a new set of bounties, for their actions in Enies Lobby.
  • Thriller Bark: Defeated Gecko Moria and freed all the shadows he stole from numerous individuals (including some of their own). The eighth crew member joins the Straw Hat Pirates, the musician, Brook.
  • Sabaody Archipelago: Attacked a World Noble, ended up freeing the slaves in the Auction House, and sent the entire island in a panic. As a result, Admiral Kizaru arrived, the Straw Hats were scattered by Bortholomew Kuma, and 500 pirates were captured. This was because they were trying to save Camie from being sold into slavery and Hatchan was shot by Charlos, which angered Luffy since he now considered Hatchan as a friend.
  • Amazon Lily: Managed to prevent Hancock from killing three of her warriors and covering her sister Sandersonia's back to prevent her slave mark from being exposed. Wanting to save Ace from execution, Luffy convinced Hancock (who had fallen in love with him) to comply to the World Government's demand she originally ignored to go to war against Whitebeard; by doing so, he prevented her from losing her Shichibukai title and the treaty to protect Amazon Lily that goes with it. This also saved Boa Hancock's life.
  • Impel Down:Invaded the deep sea prison Impel Down and created a massive breakout that brought disgrace to Impel Down. Freed dangerous prisoners like Crocodile, Jinbei, Ivankov, Daz Bones etc.
  • Marineford: Invaded Marineford and got involved in the Whitebeard War, had his heritage revealed, succeeded in releasing Ace but failed to save his life (or rather Ace saved him from being killed by Akainu), and falling into a mental collapse before being evacuated from the area.
  • After the war: Snuck into Marineford a second time and rang the Ox Bell 16 times. This was, however, a diversion to the real message: a temporary tattoo on his arm which informed his crew to meet up in two years, not three days as was originally planned. His actions during the war earned him a new bounty
  • Time Skip: The Straw Hat Pirates plan to meet up in 2 years and all of them dedicate themselves to training to face the New World.
  • Reunion to Sabaody Archipelago: After 2 years have passed, the Straw Hats come back into the world spotlight. Sanji, Luffy and Zoro showcase their strength against a couple of Pacifista's simply because it was in the way and their re-appearance exposed the Fake Straw Hat Crew.
  • Fish-Man Island: Intervened in a coup 'd'Ã©tat staged by the New Fish-Man Pirates and the Flying Pirates, defeating them and saving the Neptune Royal Family and the island. Later, having befriended the citizens of Fish-Man Island, Luffy declared war on Big Mom and her crew for ownership of Fish-Man Island. They attempted to recruit Jinbe, who refused, saying that he has some business to take care of before he is able to join them.

New World

  • Punk Hazard: Straw Hats ally with the new Shichibukai, Trafalgar Law, in order to defeat one of the four emperors, Kaido. Together with Samurai from Wano and Vice-Admiral Smoker and the G-5 soldiers they defeat and capture Caesar Clown and destry the SAD factory. They anger Donquixote Doflamingo by holding Caesar for ransom and destroying his SMILE devil fruit business.
  • Dressrosa:: Defeated Donquixote Doflamingo and saved Dressrosa from a decade long tyrrany. The Straw hat Grand Fleet is formed in secret. Sabo meets Luffy and eats Ace's devil fruit as a memento of Ace. Due to the defeat of Doflamingo, all his customers in the black market divert their anger to the Straw Hats.
  • Zou: The Straw Hats save the country of Zou from Jack. Sanji was blackmailed to attend his political marriage. The existence of Road Poneglyphs is revealed and an alliance is formed with the Mink Tribe and the Samurai of Wano with the common goal of defeating Kaido and the traitorous Shogun of Wano.
  • Totto Land: The Sanji Retrieval Team invades the territory of one of the four emperors, Big Mom, to save Sanji from his forced political marriage. They successfully convince Sanji to come back. The Straw Hats then ally with the Sun Pirates and the Fire Tank Pirates in order to assassinate Big Mom and save Sanji's family. The assassination of Big Mom is unsuccesful but they manage to save Germa and successfully escape from Big Mom's territory. During the raid of Totto Land they manage to copy Big Mom's Road Poneglyph and defeat 2 of the 3 Sweet Generals of the Big Mom Pirates including the billion berry man Katakuri.
